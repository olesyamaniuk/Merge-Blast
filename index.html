FAQ

  • How do I start playing Woody Blast?

    Download the game from the Play Store and tap 'Play' to begin matching blocks.

  • Is this game free?

    Yes, with optional in-app purchases for special items and boosters.

  • Can I play offline? 

    Absolutely! Woody Blast is designed to be enjoyable anywhere.

  • How do I earn rewards?

    Complete levels, log in daily, and participate in special events to earn rewards.

  • What makes this game unique?

    The soothing wooden theme and strategic gameplay make it both relaxing and challenging.
